Our Instructors

Understanding our clients' needs.

We are only as good as our instructors.


We have a small but special group of Pilates instructors. While they re all STOTT PILATES-trained, Pure Pilates instructors are continually in learning mode. We place significant emphasis on exposing our instructors to the same pilates training programmes so that they are able to maintain and apply a consistent approach and technique to their teachings.


Pure Pilates experienced instructors are encouraged to understand as much as they can about their students. By displaying an empathetic approach to their students needs, they form a closer and hopefully longer-lasting relationship with them.

Bob Smith - Owner

Bob start Pure Pilates with the intention of providing a relaxed, supportive environment for people with little or no previous Pilates experience.

With over 10 years' experience as a STOTT PILATES-trained instructor, Bob loves waking up every day knowing that he's going to be making a difference to someone's life today. Whether it's explaining the benefits of Pilates to a prospective student, or running the advanced class, Bob will make sure that all his student's recieve a first-class experience.

Jill Jones

Jill was born and raised in the States and moved to New Zealand once she realised that the kiwi lifestyle is much more compatible with Pilates.

Jill will help you escape the hustle and bustle of everyday life and realise your true potential for living a healthy lifestyle. Like Bob, Jill is a STOTT PILATES-trained instructor and loves sharing her knowledge, either one on one or within a class environment.

Patty Petal

Patty is our resident hippy, originating from Titirangi in West Auckland, Patty loves being surrounded by nature and her favourite leisure activity is meditating by waterfalls.

Like all our instructors, Patty is STOTT PILATES-trained, and has been instructing at Pure Pilates for two years. While Patty is our junior instructor, she still has a wealth of knowledge to divulge to all our students.
