What Our Students Say.

Pilates is an addiction for many of our students.

"I THOUGHT IT WAS ONLY FOR FEMALES AND SOFTIES"

Being a builder ( and ex-rugby player ) with on-going lower back pain and re-occuring aches and pains, I needed to try something different because the continued visits to physio and the osteopath just weren't doing it. I had heard a little about Pilates over the years, but like most males, I formed the attitude that it was simply for females and softies. How could it be of benefit and challenging for me. Well Bob and his team have certainly proved me wrong because it is.


What's the saying? - "don't knock it 'til you've tried it" - it is so true. I'm just so pleased I gave it a go, I've never felt better, more flexible and fitter. Those constant aches and pains are a thing of the past. The only one not happy is my osteopath.


Jim Wood - Builder

"A BIG FACTOR IN KEEPING ME OUT ON THE TRACK"

From struggling to survive an hour with Bob, to now feeling as though an hour isn't long enough, I can't imagine my training regime without Pure Pilates.


The addition of Pilates into my training schedule has had incredible benefits for my career as an athlete. Pilates has improved my efficiency of movement and has isolated and strengthened muscular deficiencies that had previously been undetected. Bob's expertise has reprogrammed my muscle firing patterns to engage the correct muscles required to execute a movement. This has been a huge asset in injury prevention. Pilates provides the perfect balance between challenge and relaxation through the use of a variety of equipment.


Sally Swift - New Zealand Track & Field Athlete
